Who Defines Truth?

This project explores the tension between faith, interpretation, and control through a personal lens. Inspired by my experiences growing up in Iran and later moving to the United Kingdom, I examine the contrast between religious principles and their societal enforcement. Using three.js, I created a 3D model of a veiled woman surrounded by darkness, with light rotating around her and Arabic scripture encircling the figure. The piece reflects a sense of confinement, suggesting how systems of power can shape and restrict identity. It questions who defines religious truth, and how something intended as guidance can become a tool of control.
